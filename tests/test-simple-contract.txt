SIMPLE LOAN AGREEMENT

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

In this Agreement, unless the context otherwise requires:

(a) "Borrower" means the party receiving the loan
(b) "Lender" means the party providing the loan
(c) "Principal Amount" means the sum being borrowed

2. LOAN TERMS AND CONDITIONS

The principal amount of the loan shall be One Million Dollars ($1,000,000).

The borrower agrees to repay the loan in accordance with the terms set forth herein.

3. INTEREST RATE AND PAYMENT

Interest shall accrue at a rate of five percent (5%) per annum.

Payments shall be due on the first day of each month.

4. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

In the event of default, the Lender may:
(a) Accelerate the loan
(b) Pursue legal remedies
(c) Charge additional fees

5. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
